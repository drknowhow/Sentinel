Features

Everything you need for web QA

Sentinel replaces scattered tools with one lightweight extension that lives in your browser's side panel.

●

Action Recording

Captures clicks, typing, scrolls, form submissions, and navigation automatically with intelligent noise reduction — debouncing, deduplication, and layout container filtering.

▶

Session Playback

Replay recorded sessions at 0.5x to 4x speed. Step-by-step mode lets you pause after each action with element highlighting so you can inspect state.

☑

Assertion Testing

Add assertions to any step — visible, hidden, text-contains, text-equals, has-class, or exists. Get a clear pass/fail report with actual vs. expected values.

📖

Visual Guide Export

Turn recordings into polished, standalone HTML guides with embedded screenshots. Edit titles, reorder steps, add notes, and share via email or Slack.

🐛

Bug Tracking

Auto-capture console errors, unhandled exceptions, failed network requests, and CSP violations. Annotate them as bug reports with severity levels.

🎥

Video Recording

Capture up to 5 minutes of video per clip as WebM. Download clips or attach them to your bug reports for unambiguous reproduction steps.

🔒

100% Local & Private

Sentinel makes zero network requests. No analytics, no telemetry, no external servers. Your sessions, screenshots, and reports never leave your machine unless you explicitly export them.

How It Works

Four steps to better QA

No setup, no accounts, no configuration files. Install, open the side panel, and start recording.

1

Record

Click the record button and interact with your web app. Sentinel captures every action with smart selectors and screenshots.

2

Review

Browse your recorded steps in the side panel. Each step shows what happened, where, and when — with a screenshot.

3

Test

Add assertions, then play back the session. Sentinel simulates every action and evaluates your assertions, producing a test report.

4

Export

Generate a standalone HTML guide, an issue report, or download a video clip. Share with your team — no logins needed.

Use Cases

Built for teams who ship

Whether you're a solo dev or part of a QA team, Sentinel fits your workflow.

QA & Testing

Record user flows, add assertions, and run regression playback. Export test reports as evidence that critical paths work before every release.

Onboarding & Documentation

Walk through your app once and export a visual step-by-step guide. New team members get screenshots and descriptions instead of outdated wikis.

Bug Reproduction

Enable error tracking, reproduce the bug, and export an issue report with automatic screenshots, console errors, and network failures attached.

Client Handoff

Record a walkthrough of completed features and export a branded HTML guide. Clients see exactly what was built — no staging environment needed.
