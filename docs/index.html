Features

Everything you need for web QA

Sentinel replaces scattered tools with one lightweight extension that lives in your browser's side panel.

Action Recording

Captures clicks, typing, scrolls, form submissions, and navigation automatically with intelligent noise reduction and smart selectors.

Session Playback

Replay recorded sessions at 0.5x to 4x speed. Step-by-step mode lets you pause after each action with element highlighting.

Assertion Testing

Add assertions to any step — visible, hidden, text-contains, text-equals, has-class, or exists. Get a clear pass/fail test report.

Visual Guide Export

Turn recordings into polished, standalone HTML guides with embedded screenshots. Edit titles, reorder steps, and share anywhere.

Bug Tracking

Auto-capture console errors, unhandled exceptions, failed network requests, and CSP violations. Export styled issue reports.

Video Recording

Capture up to 5 minutes of video per clip as WebM. Download clips or attach them to your bug reports for clear reproduction.

100% Local & Private

Sentinel makes zero network requests. No analytics, no telemetry, no external servers. Your sessions, screenshots, and reports never leave your machine unless you explicitly export them.

How It Works

Four steps to better QA

No setup, no accounts, no configuration files. Install, open the side panel, and start recording.

1

Record

Click the record button and interact with your web app. Sentinel captures every action with smart selectors and screenshots.

2

Review

Browse your recorded steps in the side panel. Each step shows what happened, where, and when — with a screenshot.

3

Test

Add assertions, then play back the session. Sentinel simulates every action and evaluates your assertions, producing a test report.

4

Export

Generate a standalone HTML guide, an issue report, or download a video clip. Share with your team — no logins needed.

Use Cases

Built for teams who ship

Whether you're a solo developer or part of a QA team, Sentinel fits your workflow.

QA Teams

Regression Testing

Record critical user flows, add assertions, and run regression playback before every release. Export test reports as evidence that paths work.

Engineering

Onboarding & Documentation

Walk through your app once and export a visual step-by-step guide. New team members get screenshots and descriptions instead of outdated wikis.

Enable error tracking, reproduce the bug, and export an issue report with screenshots, console errors, and network failures attached.

Product
